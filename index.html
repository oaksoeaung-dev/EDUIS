Quality Education, Quality Generations, Quality Community

Welcome to our EDU International School! EDU is a international school based in Myanmar, offering academic excellence in a caring environment to students mainly from Myanmar. EDU is located at Pwin Oo Lwin, a very beautiful hilly town, in Mandalay Division.

Our School's Vision

Be a sustainable international school to build a vibrant community with intellectual and innovative generations.
